## Business Problem

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations because losing experienced employees increases recruitment costs, training expenses, and decreases overall productivity. Human Resource (HR) departments need a way to identify employees who are at risk of leaving so that proactive retention strategies can be implemented.

This project aims to build a machine learning model that predicts employee attrition based on employee demographics, job-related factors, compensation, and work environment characteristics.

---

## What I'm Building

A data-driven Employee Attrition Prediction System that:

Analyzes HR employee data
Identifies key factors contributing to employee attrition
Predicts whether an employee is likely to leave the company
Provides insights through data visualizations and dashboards
Helps HR teams make informed retention decisions

## Dataset


**Dataset Name:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance
**Original File:** WA_Fn-UseC_-HR-Employee-Attrition.csv
**Local File:** hr_attrition.csv
**Source:** Kaggle

Dataset contains employee demographic, compensation, job satisfaction, and performance-related information used to predict employee attrition.

### Key Features

The dataset contains employee information such as:

Age
Gender
Department
Job Role
Monthly Income
Education
Years at Company
Job Satisfaction
Work-Life Balance
Overtime
Attrition Status (Target Variable)
